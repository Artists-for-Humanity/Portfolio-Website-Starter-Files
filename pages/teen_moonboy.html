TEEN MOONBOY

Moonboy grew a lot in length but not so much in width, he was still tiny compared to his siblings. We think it's because he ran around a LOT more (and maybe he was the runt), but he was always exploring and getting stuck in weird places.
He was very much interested in going outside and he'd escape a lot only to come running back in. He was as much of a scaredy-cat as he was curious.
